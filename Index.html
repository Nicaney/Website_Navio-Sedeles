About Sta. Cruz Island

Sta. Cruz Island, located off the coast of Zamboanga City, Philippines, is a breathtaking island paradise. It's known for its fine sand beaches with a unique pink hue and crystal-clear blue waters. Recognized by National Geographic as one of the 21 Best Beaches in the World in 2017, Sta. Cruz Island offers a variety of activities for travelers, including swimming, snorkeling, diving, and exploring the island's natural beauty.

Activities

  • ✓ Swimming in the turquoise waters
  • ✓ Snorkeling and diving among vibrant coral reefs
  • ✓ Relaxing on the famous pink sand beach
  • ✓ Exploring the island's lush vegetation and wildlife
